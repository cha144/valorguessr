Bugs/Issues

Common bug #1: Objects are distorted due to browser size. To fix this, please ensure that your browser is maximized. Additionally, if you are using Microsoft Edge, it has a taskbar on the side that will affect the appearance. Please disable this for ValorGuessr. Also note that resolving this issue as best as possible is a work in progress.

Common bug #2: Chosen location (black pointer) isn't accurate. If your browser is maximized and you are still having this issue, unfortunately there is nothing you can do at the moment as it is being fixed right now. Please be patient with it!

Thanks for understanding and have fun locating!
